About

Designing textiles that balance concept and usability.

I create textile collections through research, swatching, sampling, and refinement. My practice connects visual inspiration with material testing, producing work that feels tactile, intentional, and relevant to end use.

This template is built for designers who want a minimal, elegant portfolio on GitHub Pages without a heavy framework. Edit the copy, swap images, and publish in minutes.

Process

From research to final sample

01

Research

Mood boards, archive references, color extraction, market positioning, and tactile inspiration gathering.

02

Development

Motif building, weave planning, repeat creation, palette selection, and quick physical or digital tests.

03

Sampling

Refining scale, handle, structure, and finish through iterative sampling and material comparison.

04

Presentation

Final curation of boards, swatches, end-use mockups, and concise storytelling for portfolio or client review.
